---
title: Trig and Hyperbolic Functions
---
# Hyperbolic in terms of trig
$\cosh(z)=\cos(iz)$
$\sinh(z)=-i\sin(iz)$

# Trig in terms of Hyperbolic
$\cos(z)=\cosh(iz)$
$\sin(z)=-i\sinh(iz)$

# Inverse of sinh and cosh in terms of ln
$\cosh^{-1}(x)=ln(x+\sqrt{x^2-1})$ 
$\sinh^{-1}(x)=ln(x+\sqrt{x^2+1})$

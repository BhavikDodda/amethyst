Intro to Group Theory

Common set names:

  • N: natural numbers {1,2,…∞}
  • Z: integers {-∞,…-3,-2,-1,0,1,2…∞}
    • Z⁻={-1,-2,-3,…-∞}
    • Z⁺={1,2,3,…∞}
  • Q: rational numbers {p/q; p,q∈Z}
    • Q* = Q-{0}
  • R: real numbers
  • I: imaginary numbers
  • C: set of complex numbers ‘a+bi’
